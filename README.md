## TafheemBangla
Syed Abul A'la Mawdudi r. The written Tafhimul Quran (Tafhimul Quran Bangla) is an invaluable addition to the literature of modern exegesis. This Tafhimul Quran Bangla Tafseer is almost unmatched in presenting the Holy Quran as a complete law of human life and on the basis of this Tafhimul Quran Bangla provides solutions to all the problems of life. The Bengali Tafseer of Tafhimul Quran Tafseer is translated into many languages. Which includes our Bengali language. We aim to make this app to make Bengali translated Tafseer easily available.

>> Key features of Tafhimul Quran app are as follows:-
✔ You can search by Surah name.
✔ Important information about each Surah is given.
✔ All types of font size and font family can be changed.
✔ Required Surahs can be bookmarked.
✔ Tafseer/Annotation section can be made invisible.
✔ The literal meaning and meaning of each surah has been added.
✔ Ayat translation and commentary can be copied.
✔ Biographies of the author and translator are given.
